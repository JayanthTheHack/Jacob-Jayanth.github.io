EasySpeak

An easy to use Chrome extension to improve your interactivity with the browser you love.

Watch your speech transform into text right before your eyes.

Our extension also listens for special commands, making browsing more interactive

Commands

  • "Open" + (webaddress) - Allows you to open a web browser (Must only be two words)

  • "Buy" + (items) - Opens Amazon with item results

  • "Search" + (queries) - Searches on Google's engine

Privacy Statement

  • The extension is only recording when there is a red light flashing on the browser tab and extension microphone

  • We do not save any data nor recordings
